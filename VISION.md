# Lumina

## Mission
Help people express what matters most.

## Vision
Become the world's most thoughtful experience engine for meaningful moments.

## What Lumina Does
Lumina transforms heartfelt thoughts into beautifully guided digital experiences.

## Core Principles

1. Emotion before decoration.
2. Reflection before presentation.
3. Technology should disappear.
4. Every experience should feel personal.
5. The engine creates emotion. Design reveals it.
6. Move thoughtfully. Build things that last.
7. We do not rush meaningful moments.

## North Star
Every Lumina experience should help someone feel appreciated.
